Frequently Asked Questions

If you have questions about how we can assist you in navigating the foreclosure process or reclaiming your assets, you're in the right place. Discover more about our expertise, the steps involved in our services, and how we can tailor our assistance to meet your unique needs.

  • What is foreclosure recovery?

    Foreclosure recovery is the process by which individuals or families who have experienced foreclosure on their homes work towards regaining financial stability and housing security.

  • What are my options if I'm facing foreclosure?

    When facing foreclosure, you can pursue options such as loan modification, refinancing, forbearance, short sale, or deed in lieu of foreclosure to avoid losing your home. Seeking legal advice from Lyke Brothers and exploring these alternatives promptly can help mitigate the impact of foreclosure and protect your financial interests.

  • How can Lyke Brothers help me with foreclosure recovery?

    Lyke Brothers specializes in foreclosure recovery, offering a range of services to assist you. We negotiate with lenders on your behalf to modify loan terms, facilitate short sales to avoid foreclosure, and explore alternative financing options. Our team provides counseling and financial education to empower you, and we offer personalized assistance tailored to your specific needs.
